Hello! My name is Ivy Rose, and I'm an 18-year-old aspiring software developer currently living in the beautiful Mediterranean island of Malta. As a student at MCAST, I’m diving deep into the world of iGaming—a field that not only fascinates me but also presents a myriad of opportunities to unite technology and entertainment. With every line of code I write and every project I complete, I feel a little closer to my dream of becoming a successful software developer.

Growing up in Malta, I’ve been fortunate to be surrounded by a rich culture and an incredible community. Living in a place where the history meets modernity has inspired me to appreciate both the roots of technology and its future potential. The vibrant atmosphere motivates me daily, encouraging me to not only embrace my education but to explore new ideas and concepts in the tech world. Whether it’s coding, game design, or learning about user experience—every aspect is a thrilling puzzle I’m eager to solve.

Family has always played a significant role in my life, providing me with the support and encouragement I need to chase my goals. They are my biggest cheerleaders, rooting for me throughout my academic journey and reminding me of the importance of hard work and dedication. My parents have instilled in me the values of resilience and passion, which I carry into every project I undertake. Their belief in my abilities fuels my ambition to succeed and make them proud.

At this stage in my life, I’m not only focused on my studies but also on the kind of future I want to build for myself. I dream of a career where I can make a difference through technology—creating innovative applications that enhance the lives of users. Software development is a constantly evolving field, and I am excited about its endless possibilities. I love the idea of contributing to projects that not only entertain but also connect people and bring them together in meaningful ways.

When I’m not busy with my studies or coding, you can find me exploring the stunning landscapes of Malta or connecting with friends over coffee, exchanging ideas and inspirations. I believe that creativity often blossoms in casual moments, so I make it a priority to unwind and recharge. Whether it’s taking a hike along the coastline or simply enjoying time with loved ones, I aim to maintain a balanced lifestyle that nurtures both my personal and professional aspirations.

As I continue my journey in the iGaming industry, I’m eager to learn, grow, and ultimately make my mark in the tech world. With ambition in my heart and a supportive family by my side, I am determined to achieve my goals and look forward to the adventures each day brings. Thank you for joining me on this exciting journey as I strive towards a successful future!
